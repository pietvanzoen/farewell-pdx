Friends & Family! Please join us as we raise a glass to our lives in Portland, and to our new adventures in the Netherlands. Bonus! It also happens to be our 7th wedding anniversary!

We will be partying all night at the Migration Brewing Annex on NE Glisan. We will have our own private bar, so come thirsty! There is also a full pub food menu if you're hungry. Kids are welcome too until 10PM. This is an open invitation, so please feel free to bring anyone along that you think would like to be there.

This move is happening so fast, and we know we regretfully won't have a chance to see you all individually before we go. But it would ease our aching hearts to see you all laughing and kicking up your heels in one place. So please join us for one last (for now) Portland hoorah!

Love, Piet & Keven
